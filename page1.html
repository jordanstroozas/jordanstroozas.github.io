Why is it the best?

I know that most of us would be quick to agree with the statement that Morbius is the best Marvel movie to be made. Mind you I be so bold to claim that Morbious is one of the greatest movies of all time, this film is truly a cinematic masterpiece from start to finish. This movie may have not been received well by some viewers, however; the sound of many inspired a sweep. Sony & Marvel to bring it back to theatre's a 2nd time which coined the term Morbius Sweep. My favorite moment in the film is when Morbius said, "It's morbin time". Director's did an exceptional job pulling the emotions out of the hearts of many.
