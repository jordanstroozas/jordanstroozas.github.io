The Greatest Movie of all time: Morbius

Why is it the best?

I know that most of us would be quick to agree with the statement that Morbius is the best Marvel movie. My favorite moment in the film is when Morbius said, "It's morbin time". Director's did an exceptional job pulling the emotions out of the hearts of many.
